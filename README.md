# EcoClean

**Background/Introduction**
Barangay Sto. Cristo in the city of Angeles, as with most urban barangays, also encounters issues with proper waste disposal. As the population expands with the process of urbanization, waste generation also rises, with littering, illegal dumping, and unsegregated garbage being the improper disposal methods that lead to pollution of the environment and sanitation issues.

Although they have waste collection systems, most of the citizens lack information regarding collection schedules, waste sorting regulations, and available sanitation programs. Inefficiency in waste management due to the lack of information leads to garbage cans overflowing, the deterioration of the environment, and health impacts.

To address these issues, EcoClean, an online platform, is proposed as an electronic solution to promote proper waste disposal in Barangay Sto. Cristo. Through garbage collection schedules, guides to proper segregation, reporting, and announcements of clean-up events, the platform aims to enhance the participation of the people in a clean and sustainable community.

**Purpose/Description of the Project:**
EcoClean is a simple community-based waste management web application designed to assist Barangay Sto. Cristo, Angeles City in promoting proper waste disposal and environmental awareness. The project aims to provide residents with an accessible platform where they can view garbage collection schedules, learn proper waste segregation, report waste-related issues, and participate in barangay clean-up drives.

**Initial Components of the Project**
1.	Garbage Collection Schedule
o	Displays the waste collection schedule per area.
o	Uses a simple calendar or list view for easy access.
2.	Waste Segregation Guide
o	Provides educational materials on biodegradable, non-biodegradable, and recyclable waste.
o	Uses icons and color coding for better understanding.
3.	Report Waste Issues
o	A form where residents can report uncollected garbage, illegal dumping, or sanitation concerns.
o	Includes an option to attach images (if using Firebase).
4.	Community Clean-Up Drives
o	Displays upcoming clean-up drives and environmental events.
o	Allows residents to sign up as volunteers.
